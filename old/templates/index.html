Hexastorm develops an open hardware fast high resolution LASER suited for Printed Circuit Board (PCB) manufacturing or 3D printing. The laser head uses a rotating prism instead of the industry standard rotating mirror. The technology is ideally suited for laser diodes. The current focus is to create more sustainable textile with lasers. By locally applying heat, the absorption of dye into the textile can be tuned. A video of the scanner can be seen on Youtube. A blog with recent progress and technical details can be found on Hackaday.
